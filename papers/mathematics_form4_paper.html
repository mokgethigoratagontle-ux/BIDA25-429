Form 4 Practice Examination Paper
MATHEMATICS
Time Allowed: 2 Hours Total Marks: 100

Instructions to Candidates:

  • Write your full name in the spaces provided at the top of this sheet.
  • Answer ALL questions in this paper.
  • Write your answers and show your working clearly in the space provided.
  • Calculators may be used where appropriate.
  • Electronic devices and mobile phones are strictly prohibited.
Question 1
Solve the following quadratic equation using the quadratic formula, showing all steps of your working:
3x² - 10x - 8 = 0
[8 Marks]
Question 2
A straight line passes through the points A(2, 5) and B(6, 13).
(a) Find the gradient (slope) of the line AB. [3 Marks]
(b) Determine the equation of the line AB, giving your answer in the form y = mx + c. [4 Marks]
Question 3
In a right-angled triangle PQR, the right angle is at Q. The side PQ = 9 cm and QR = 12 cm.
(a) Calculate the length of the hypotenuse PR, showing your use of Pythagoras' theorem. [4 Marks]
(b) Find the value of sin(∠QPR) and cos(∠QPR) in their simplest fraction forms. [4 Marks]
Question 4
Simplify the following algebraic expression completely:
((2x² - 8) / (x - 2)) × (3 / (x + 2))
[8 Marks]
Question 5
The table below shows the distribution of scores for 40 students in a physics quiz:
Score Range 1 - 5 6 - 10 11 - 15 16 - 20
No. of Students 5 12 15 8
(a) State the modal score interval. [2 Marks]
(b) Estimate the mean score of the class to one decimal place, using class midpoints. [7 Marks]
