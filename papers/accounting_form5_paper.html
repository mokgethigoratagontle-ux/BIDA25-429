Form 5 Practice Examination Paper
ACCOUNTING
Time Allowed: 2 Hours Total Marks: 100

Instructions to Candidates:

  • Write your full name in the spaces provided at the top of this sheet.
  • Answer ALL questions in this paper.
  • Use standard double-entry accounting principles in all ledgers and balancing.
  • Show all calculations clearly, as marks are awarded for intermediate steps.
  • All currency units are in Botswana Pula (P).
Question 1 (Ledger Accounts)
Record the following business transactions for "Mpho Retailers" for the month of April 2026 into the T-accounts (Cash Account, Capital Account, and Purchases Account) using the double-entry system.
• April 1: Mpho started business with P50,000 cash.
• April 5: Bought goods for resale paying P12,500 by cash.
• April 12: Purchased office furniture on credit from Woodworks Ltd for P8,000.
• April 20: Paid Woodworks Ltd P5,000 by cash as part-payment.
[10 Marks]
Question 2 (Trial Balance)
The ledger balances of "Lekgolo & Sons" on 31 December 2025 were as follows:
Capital: P85,000 | Sales: P120,000 | Purchases: P75,000 | Cash at Bank: P24,000 | Rent Expense: P12,000 | Accounts Receivable: P32,000 | Accounts Payable: P18,000 | Equipment: P60,000.
Prepare a standard Trial Balance as of 31 December 2025, showing clearly the debit and credit column totals. [10 Marks]
Question 3 (Adjusting Entries & Depreciation)
A business bought a delivery van on 1 January 2023 for P150,000. The business uses the reducing balance method of depreciation at a rate of 20% per annum.
(a) Calculate the depreciation expense and net book value of the delivery van for Year 1 (2023) and Year 2 (2024). [5 Marks]
(b) Record the journal entry for the depreciation expense at 31 December 2024. [5 Marks]
Question 4 (Three-Column Cash Book)
Prepare a Three-Column Cash Book (Cash, Bank, and Discount columns) for "Bosele Traders" from the following details, and balance it at the end of the month:
• May 1: Balances brought forward: Cash P3,500, Bank P15,200.
• May 8: Paid rent by cheque P1,200.
• May 15: Received cheque from J. Brown for P2,850 in full settlement of his debt of P3,000.
• May 22: Cash sales deposited directly into bank P4,500.
• May 29: Paid cash P800 to K. Black, who allowed us a cash discount of P50.
[15 Marks]
Question 5 (Income Statement & Balance Sheet Structure)
State the basic Accounting Equation and explain the difference between Current Assets and Non-Current Assets, providing two examples of each in your description. [10 Marks]
